The Vikings travelled far and wide during the Viking Age (793 AD–1066 AD), and had a major impact on cultures and languages across Europe and further afield. Their exploits were mainly written up in sagas (the best known is the Heimskringla, the sagas of the Viking kings, compiled by the Icelander Snorri Sturluson around 1230 AD). But how well do you actually know your Vikings?

In this light-hearted game you will be shown an image or question linked to the Vikings, and your task is to match the fact (or Viking) to the related tile on a grid. Some tiles have written facts, and some have illustrations.

Are you a Viking scholar? If you think you are, then click Start!
